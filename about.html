About Luke Lava

I like photography and programming. This website is a way to combine those two interests.

I wanted a way to test a way of uploading photos that could protect against webscraping. So, I split the images into 3 channels and overlayed them on one another. If everything is working right, you shouldn't be able to tell any difference unless you try opening an image in a new tab.

If you want photos taken, contact me on instagram please.
